Wrong Use ( Without NoIndent )
In this wrong use case, there are two Container50 boxes for dividing of area to fifty fifty. On the left side, gray bordered Container50 has a Card element inside and Card element pushed to inwards with indentation of Container50. And on the right side, a red dash bordered ContainerX divided to two area with two Container50 boxes which have a Card elements. On the right side a red dashed bordered ContainerX has not NoIndent class. As shown, purple dash bordered Container50 boxes pushing to inwards with indentation together Cards. Therefore, right on the cards in the card on the left where you can not get the same top level and the layout does not appear correctly. In addition, the distance between the boxes are not equal.

Proper Use ( With NoIndent )
In this proper use case, on the right side a blue dashed bordered ContainerX has NoIndent class. As shown, orange dash bordered Container50 boxes are not pushing to inwards with indentation together Cards. Therefore, right on the cards in the card on the left where you can get the same top level and the layout does appear correctly and of course the distance between the boxes are equal.
